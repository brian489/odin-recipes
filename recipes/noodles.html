Noodles

Description :

This is a recipe for instant noodles by me. It takes a total 5 mins and serves 1 person.

Ingredients:

  • mie goreng packet
  • water
  • Optional: 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Boil water until water is boiling
  2. Add noodles to a bowl and pour hot water onto noodles, letting them cook in the water for atleast 2 mins
  3. Add seasoning packets to the noodles and mix until thourghly mixed
  4. optional: Cook an egg and add it to the noodles
  5. Enjoy
